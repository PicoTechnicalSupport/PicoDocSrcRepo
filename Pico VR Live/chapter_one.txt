1 Applicability
===============================

This video player is applicable for playing on-demand, live, local and online video files. The specific features are as follows:

- Playing video files saved on the Internet (HTTP) server.

- Playing local saved video files.

- Playing online and live video streaming that follows the RTMP protocol.

- Playing online and live video streaming that follows the RTSP protocol.

Playing online and live This video player is applicable for playing on-demand, live, local and online video files. The specific features are as follows:

- video streaming that follows the RTMP protocol.

- This player supports custom video posters, logo, and application background.
