6	Reset Launcher to PicoVRlauncher
=========================================================

Here are the steps to reset launcher to PicoVRlauncher,

- Step1: Access the 2D settings menu by pressing the Confirm button and the Volume Down button on the headset at the same time.

- Step2: Select Apps & notifications->advanced->Default apps->Home app->choose PVRLauncher, then reboot the headset.
