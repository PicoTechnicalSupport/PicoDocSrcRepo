9 	Known Issues
====================================

9.1 VR Preview
------------------

The SDK doesn’t support **VR Preview** in the editor.

.. image:: _static/9.1.png    

Figure 9.1 - VR Preview is not supported in the editor.

9.2 Build Support
------------------

Before packaging, navigate to **Edit** > **Project Settings** > **Platform** > **Android** and disable **Enable Gradle instead of Ant**:

.. image:: _static/9.2.png    

Figure 9.2 - Disable Enable Gradle instead of Ant.
